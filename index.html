HOW TO PLAY

This is a text-based game, where you type in the decisions and actions that are available to. Depending on your decisions, it can lead to a variety of outcomes of the game. There are clues and hints in the game, so read the text carefully before you make a choice. But don't take too long making a choice as every second that goes by the storm gets closer.

WHAT WENT WILL
  • Planning - Before beginning the project, we planned what the project would be about and its story. With this we had a clear idea of the structure of the project, what it would need and where.
  • Communication - All members kept in constant communication periodically, to either update about the progress that has been made about the work or to lend aid to the other in solving a problem. This leaded to effective time management.
  • Task Management - The tasks were distributed evenly amongst the group members. At times member would then delegate to the tasks that they were most comfortable with or a better understanding of how it would work. After the main body or structure of the game was developed, further detail was then added to further refine the game all while carrying out tests for any bugs or errors.

ABOUT GAME

Takes place in the outskirts of the forest, you are an Ogre waking up one evening to find that his donkey is no were to be found from his trip outside. Whats more there is a large and terrifying storm approaching. The Ogre needs to go out and search for his pet donkey before the storm arrives.
